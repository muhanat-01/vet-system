## Project Overview

**Happy Farmer** is a web-based system designed to improve access to veterinary services for rural farmers. The platform provides **immediate livestock health feedback** using **AI-based symptom analysis**, structured communication, and digital record management. The goal is to reduce delays in veterinary assistance, improve animal health outcomes, and empower farmers with timely, data-driven insights.

This project focuses on solving real challenges faced by rural farmers, including long wait times for vets, lack of proper health records, and limited access to expert advice.

---

## Problem Statement

Rural farmers often face:

* Delayed access to veterinary professionals
* Poor or non-existent livestock medical records
* Difficulty describing animal symptoms accurately
* High livestock losses due to late diagnosis

Happy Farmer addresses these challenges through technology-enabled, farmer-friendly solutions.

---

## Key Features

* **AI Symptom Analysis**
  Farmers input livestock symptoms and receive instant health feedback with up to **85% analysis accuracy**.

* **Reduced Vet Response Time**
  Cuts waiting time for veterinary assistance by up to **40%**.

* **Livestock Health Records Management**
  Centralized database for storing animal profiles, medical history, treatments, and follow-ups.

* **User-Centered Design**
  Simple and intuitive interface tailored for rural farmers with minimal technical skills.
